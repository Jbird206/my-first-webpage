Vitoria-Gastiez, Basque Country, Spain
 

Vitoria-Gasteiz is the Capitol of the Basque region. The language, Basque, is a language isolate. Meaning that nowhere else in the world speaks it, as well as it not stemming from an already established language. Historically the origins of Basque seems entirely uncertain. Even though Basque country is a region of Spain, it has its own culture, food and traditions.
